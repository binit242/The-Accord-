What is Accord?

Accord is a smart contact manager designed to give you full control over your connections. It securely stores your contacts in the cloud, making them accessible anytime, anywhere. With built-in support for social profiles, Accord helps you manage not just phone numbers and emails, but the full digital identity of your network — all in one intuitive, centralized platform.
